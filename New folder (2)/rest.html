About Rutti's Restauarant

Red Fort offers an exquisite dining experience inspired by the rich flavors and traditions of India. With our family-owned roots, we pride ourselves on serving authentic Indian dishes that capture the essence of our heritage.

Our chefs are committed to bringing the finest spices, fresh ingredients, and time-honored techniques to each plate, offering you a taste of India’s diverse culinary landscape.
